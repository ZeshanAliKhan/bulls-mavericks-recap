Bulls vs Mavericks recap: Dallas turned the finale into a stat night.

Dallas beat Chicago 149-128 at American Airlines Center. The clean headline is Ryan Nembhard's 23 assists, but the full box score tells a bigger story: 22 Mavericks threes, 97 Dallas bench points, and a Chicago side that still found a few interesting lines in a lopsided loss.

The game in one read

Dallas opened with 45 first-quarter points and never really let the night settle into a normal rhythm. Chicago kept scoring in the paint and finished with 128, but it could not match the Mavericks' shooting volume from three.

The box score makes the split obvious: Chicago made 10 threes. Dallas made 22. That twelve-three gap is a 36-point swing before you even get into second-chance points or bench production.
